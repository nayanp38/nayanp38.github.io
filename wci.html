Purpose - Project Global Surgery

In conjunction with Operation Smile, we are working to improve medical infrastructure in Madagascar to perform critical surgical procedures.

Currently, we are developing a library of instructional recordings of life-saving procedures to help train new Malagasy physicians.


(Left: Dr. Sanjay Daluvoy demonstrating a head-mounted GoPro to record a surgery.)

I have been working to address technological shortcomings of the GoPro recording setup - overheating, lack of battery capacity, and low memory. One of such modifications is a 3D printed door replacement for the GoPro Hero 9 that allows it to charge while in use, allowing for continuous recording in sterile surgical environments.
